Less time at the vendor!

Scrap sells all your junk when you visit a merchant. It is designed to be easy to use and set which items to sell. And it comes with even more features:

• Intelligent junk filters.
• Tags junk items in inventory.
• Can automatically repair your equipment.
• Many available plugins for extra functionality.
• In-game tutorials.

 

Plugin Examples

A wide assortment of plugins has been made by people in the community, such as Bagnon, ElvUI and DataBroker integration, or an automatic inventory cleaner.
